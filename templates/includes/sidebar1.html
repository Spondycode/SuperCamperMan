Super Campista !

Super Camper is a community of campers who share camping plot information with other campers. It's a place to find and record the best camping spots. Next time you want to find your plot or your special wild camping spot, you can find it here.

Inbox

Have a look at the Inbox - A place to contact other SuperCampers. Send a direct message to your friends and they will see next time they log in. Or they will see directly if already logged in.

Like - Comment - Reply

Comment on any of the plots posted - Another way to make contact with other SuperCamper friends. Reply to comments and add Likes to plots, comments and replies.
